About UNIBASE

UNIBASE is dedicated to memes, with its branding, community engagement, and platform activities centered around popular internet memes and meme culture.

The project encourages active participation through meme creation contests, meme-themed events, and interactive social media campaigns. Community members can earn tokens for their contributions and engagement.

On future Unibase features as a decentralized marketplace, users can buy and sell meme-related digital assets, such as rare meme NFTs (Non-Fungible Tokens) and meme-themed merchandise.

Built on Basechain, Unibase benefits from its scalability and low transaction fees, ensuring smooth and cost-effective meme transactions and community interactions.
